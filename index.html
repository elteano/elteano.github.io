CSE 167 at UCSD:

Final project, a collaboration with my peer Marco Mendez. I personally created the bloom effects and the water effects.

image here

The bloom effects are implemented using a Gaussian blur over the highlights of the scene. First, the items are rendered as normal; they are then filtered for the highlights, which are extracted into a separate framebuffer. The highlights are then flipped back and forth between two framebuffers, each time blurring either on the horizontal or vertical axes.
